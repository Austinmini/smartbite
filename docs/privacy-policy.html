Privacy Policy - SavvySpoon

Last updated: April 2026

SavvySpoon ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create a SavvySpoon account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Name (optional)
  • Account creation date

1.2 Profile & Preference Data

To provide personalized meal plans and shopping recommendations, we collect:

  • Weekly food budget
  • Dietary goals and preferences
  • Food allergies and restrictions
  • Cuisine preferences
  • Preferred grocery stores and chains
  • Cooking time preferences
  • Location (zip code, latitude, longitude) for store proximity and geo-restriction

1.3 Purchase & Behavior Data

We collect information about your activities in the app:

  • Meal plans generated and recipes viewed
  • Saved recipes and collections
  • Price scans submitted (item UPC, price, quantity, store, timestamp)
  • Purchases logged (item name, quantity, unit, price, store)
  • Pantry items and quantities
  • Recipes marked as cooked and frequency
  • Price alerts and reminders created
  • Ratings and notes on saved recipes

1.4 Location Data

We request access to your device's location to:

  • Auto-populate nearby grocery stores (on-device calculation; location is not transmitted)
  • Verify you are in the Texas launch region (geo-restriction enforcement)
  • Calculate store distances for shopping list optimization

You can grant or deny location permission in your device settings. The app functions without location (store distance will not be calculated), but geo-restriction checks will not be possible.

1.5 Analytics & Usage Data

We use PostHog to track:

  • Key actions (meal plan generation, recipe saves, scans, purchases, subscriptions)
  • Error tracking and crash analytics
  • Session duration and frequency
  • App version and device type

This data is anonymized and aggregated unless you are logged in. When logged in, we tie analytics to your user ID for engagement insights.

1.6 Error & Crash Reporting

We use Sentry to collect information about app crashes and errors, including:

  • Stack traces and error messages
  • Device OS and app version
  • User context (user ID when authenticated)

1.7 Subscription & Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by RevenueCat, which connects to Apple App Store and Google Play. We do NOT store credit card data. RevenueCat handles:

  • Subscription product identifiers
  • Purchase dates and renewal status
  • Trial status and entitlements

See RevenueCat's privacy policy for their data handling: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

1.8 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Health or medical information (despite recipe nutrition data)
  • Age or date of birth (unless you voluntarily provide it)
  • Credit card or payment information (handled by Apple/Google/RevenueCat)
  • Full address (only zip code for store proximity)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Generate personalized meal plans via AI (Anthropic Claude API)
  • Display real-time grocery prices from community scans
  • Match your preferences to recipe recommendations
  • Store and retrieve your pantry, purchase history, and saved recipes
  • Track your scanning activity and award Bites currency
  • Send push notifications for reminders, price drops, and trials ending
  • Improve the app through usage analytics
  • Fix bugs and security issues
  • Detect fraud and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Services

We share data with:

  • Supabase (authentication, database): https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Anthropic (meal plan AI): https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • PostHog (analytics): https://posthog.com/privacy
  • Sentry (error tracking): https://sentry.io/privacy/
  • RevenueCat (subscriptions): https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3.2 Community Data (Price Scans)

Price observations you submit are aggregated into a community database. Other users can see:

  • The price you reported (not your name or ID)
  • The store and date of the scan
  • Your "contributor score" (a reputation number that weights your reports)

Your individual scans are never publicly attributed to you by name. The community database powers pricing for all users.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law (subpoena, court order, or government request) or to protect the rights and safety of SavvySpoon users and the public.

3.4 Business Transfers

If SavvySpoon is merged, acquired, or sells assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted 90 days after account deletion.
  • Price observations: Retained indefinitely (aggregated into canonical prices).
  • Purchase history: Retained for 2 years, then anonymized.
  • Analytics: Retained for 90 days by PostHog, then deleted.
  • Error logs: Retained for 90 days by Sentry, then deleted.

5. Your Rights & Data Access

5.1 Access & Correction

You can view and update your profile information anytime in the app's Profile screen. If you need a copy of all your data, contact us at privacy@savvyspoon.app.

5.2 Data Deletion

You can delete your account in the app's Profile > Account Settings > Delete Account. This will:

  • Immediately delete your user account, profile, meal plans, and saved recipes
  • Mark your price observations and purchases as anonymized (your user ID is removed)
  • Complete deletion from all backups within 90 days

Note: Aggregated price data (canonical prices) cannot be deleted as it is community-owned and drives the product for all users.

5.3 CCPA & GDPR Rights

If you are a California or European resident, you have rights under CCPA and GDPR respectively:

  • Right to know: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to delete: Request deletion of your personal data (see Section 5.2)
  • Right to opt-out: Opt out of analytics and marketing emails
  • Right to correct: Request correction of inaccurate data

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@savvyspoon.app with "CCPA Request" or "GDPR Request" in the subject line.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Password hashing (bcrypt) and salting
  • Database encryption at rest (Supabase PostgreSQL)
  • Rate limiting on API endpoints to prevent abuse
  • JWT token-based authentication
  • No sensitive data logged (passwords, API keys)

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute protection. If you believe your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact us.

7. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for:

  • Price drop alerts (when your alert target is met)
  • Purchase reminders (based on your schedule)
  • Trial expiry warnings (24 hours before trial ends)
  • Important app updates and announcements

You can disable notifications in your device settings or the app's Profile > Notifications.

8. Children's Privacy

SavvySpoon is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us at privacy@savvyspoon.app.

9. California Privacy Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal data is collected, used, shared, and sold
  • Delete personal data collected from you (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal data
  • Non-discrimination for exercising these rights

We do not "sell" personal data as defined by CCPA. We do not use data for targeted advertising. To submit a CCPA request, contact privacy@savvyspoon.app.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Access to your personal data
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Our legal basis for processing is: (1) contract performance (app functionality), (2) legitimate interest (analytics, fraud prevention), (3) consent (marketing communications). To exercise these rights, contact privacy@savvyspoon.app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above and sending an email to your registered address. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
