**Full Description (4000 characters, use below):**
```
SavvySpoon helps you eat well within your budget by combining AI-powered meal planning with crowd-sourced grocery prices.

PERSONALIZED MEAL PLANS
Generate 7-day meal plans tailored to your dietary goals, allergies, and cooking preferences. Our AI learns from your favorite recipes and suggests meals you'll actually cook.

REAL-TIME GROCERY PRICES
Every user contributes actual store prices when they scan items at the grocery shelf. See which store has the best deal for your ingredients, and compare prices across your preferred retailers before you shop.

SMART SHOPPING
Our algorithm automatically splits your shopping list across stores to save you the most money. Track what you buy over time and get reminders to restock your staples.

COMMUNITY REWARDS
Earn Bites (our reward currency) for scanning prices and contributing to the community database. Every scan helps your neighborhood make smarter shopping decisions.

FEATURES
✓ AI-powered personalized meal plans
✓ Real-time crowd-sourced grocery prices
✓ Shopping list with automatic store optimization
✓ Barcode scanner for price reporting
✓ Pantry inventory tracking
✓ Purchase history and trends
✓ Price alerts and reminders
✓ Saved recipes and collections
✓ Works with any Texas grocery store

SUBSCRIPTION TIERS
Free: 2 meal plans per week, basic price comparison
Plus: Unlimited meal plans, price trends, price alerts ($4.99/month)
Pro: AI suggestions, purchase reminders, family profiles ($9.99/month)

New users get a 7-day free trial of Pro!

ABOUT US
SavvySpoon is built by food and technology enthusiasts who believe that eating well shouldn't break the bank. We're launching in Texas and expanding state by state.

Your privacy is our priority. We don't sell data. Community prices are aggregated and anonymized. Learn more in our Privacy Policy.
```

## Screenshot Guidelines

### What to Include

1. **Clear product focus** — App UI takes up 80%+ of screen
2. **Text overlay** — 1-2 lines explaining the feature (use bright contrast)
3. **Real data** — Use actual app screens with realistic meal names, prices, groceries
4. **Consistent style** — Same fonts, colors, branding across all screenshots
5. **Device frame** — Optionally add device bezel (many devices skip this for cleaner look)

### Tools for Creating Screenshots

1. **Simulator Screenshots**
   ```bash
   # iOS Simulator (Xcode)
   xcrun simctl io booted screenshot screenshot.png
   
   # Android Emulator
   adb shell screencap -p /sdcard/screenshot.png
   adb pull /sdcard/screenshot.png
   ```

2. **Screenshot Editors** (add text, resize, crop)
   - GIMP (free, open-source)
   - Figma (design-focused)
   - Adobe Photoshop (professional)
   - Preview.app (macOS built-in)

3. **Automated Tools**
   - Fastlane (can automate screenshot generation)
   - Detox (E2E testing tool with screenshot capability)
